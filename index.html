About Me

DJ KFIVE (Kester) is a Nigerian-Waterloo German open-format DJ with over 12 years of experience across Hannover and Niedersachsen. Known for high-energy sets and deep crowd engagement, he has performed at premium clubs, bars, and events in Germany and beyond.

Specialties include music mixing, playlist curation, live sound delivery, and cross-genre transitions covering Afrobeats, Dancehall, HipHop, RnB, Ragga, Latino (Reggaeton,, House), and more.

Certified in event pacing and vibe control, he transforms audiences with seamless blend and forward-thinking energy selection.

Skills

  • Live DJ performances (clubs + festivals + weddings)
  • Track mixing & beatmatching
  • Playlist strategy & audience programming
  • Crowd reading and energy control
  • Turntable proficiency (live scratching / transitions)
